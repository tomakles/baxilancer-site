FAQ: BAXILANCER automatic time tracking

How does automatic GPS time tracking work in BAXILANCER?

You create a project and set its address. When you arrive on site, BAXILANCER automatically starts tracking time based on your GPS location. When you leave, the timer stops and your work hours are saved to your history.

Is BAXILANCER suitable for trades and field work?

Yes. BAXILANCER is built mainly for trades, installers, technicians and anyone who works on site at the client. It helps track hours automatically and link expenses and receipts to each project.

Can I track receipts and expenses in BAXILANCER?

BAXILANCER lets you scan receipts with AI, assign them to projects and generate clear reports for invoicing and taxes, combining time tracking and expense tracking in one tool.
