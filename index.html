The Problem With Manual Time Tracking

Most contractors still track hours on paper, in spreadsheets, or by memory. The result: forgotten hours, inaccurate invoices, and lost money. After a long day running between sites, who remembers exactly when they arrived and left each one?

Manual tracking fails because it depends on you remembering to do it. When you are busy working, that rarely happens. You end up guessing, under-billing clients, and losing hours you actually put in.

BAXILANCER solves this. It runs in the background and uses GPS to log your hours automatically. When you arrive at a location, the timer starts. When you leave, it stops. No buttons to press, no timers to remember. Your hours are recorded accurately, every time.
