Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

At Baxilancer, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy applies to the use of the Baxilancer iOS Application and our website (baxilancer.ovh).

2. Data Collection and Usage

Account Information

To use the application, you must register using your email address. We use your email to authenticate you and sync your data securely to the cloud.

Location Tracking (Background Location)

Our application uses "Background Location Tracking" features. This is strictly used to provide the core functionality of the app: automatic time tracking when you arrive at or leave a designated project location (geo-fencing).

  • Usage: We detect entry and exit events to start and stop timers automatically.
  • Privacy: We do not track your location history continuously, nor do we sell your location data to third parties. Your location data is used solely for the purpose of personal time tracking.

Camera Usage

The app requires access to your device's Camera.

  • Usage: The camera is used to let you scan and photograph physical receipts for expense tracking.
  • Storage: These images are uploaded to our secure cloud storage so you can access them across your devices. We do not inspect these images for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may use third-party service providers (such as Google Firebase) to help us operate our business and the application, solely for infrastructure purposes (database, authentication, storage).

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.
