Why We Write About Time Tracking

Time tracking is one of the biggest operational challenges for contractors, tradespeople, and freelancers. Unlike office workers who sit at a desk all day, field workers move between job sites, deal with unpredictable schedules, and rarely have time for administrative tasks. That is why we publish guides and articles to help you track your hours more accurately and get paid for every minute of work.

Topics We Cover

Our blog focuses on practical, real-world advice for people who work with their hands. We cover subjects like GPS time tracking, receipt management for tax deductions, automated reporting, and tips for reducing unbilled hours. Whether you are an electrician, plumber, or HVAC technician, you will find content relevant to your specific challenges.

Solutions for Different Roles

Not every worker faces the same problems. A general contractor managing multiple crews has different needs than a solo freelancer billing hourly. That is why we also maintain dedicated landing pages for GPS time tracking for contractors, job site time tracking, and automatic time tracking for freelancers. Each page addresses the unique workflow of that role.
